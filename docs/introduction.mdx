# Welcome to SPT Teams

SPT Teams is a comprehensive enterprise management platform designed to streamline organizational operations, enhance team collaboration, and provide powerful insights through AI-driven analytics. Built for modern businesses, our platform combines advanced workspace management, robust role-based access control, and intelligent automation to help your organization thrive.

## 🎯 **What is SPT Teams?**

SPT Teams is an all-in-one enterprise platform that provides:

- **Hierarchical Multi-Workspace Management** - Organize your business with main workspaces and sub-workspaces
- **Advanced Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** - Granular permissions for owners, admins, and members
- **Intelligent Team Management** - AI-powered team formation and collaboration insights
- **Dynamic Reporting & Analytics** - Comprehensive reporting with real-time insights
- **Enterprise Document Management** - Secure file storage and collaboration
- **Financial Management Suite** - Complete expense, budget, and invoice management
- **HR Management Suite** - Employee management, payroll, attendance, and recruitment
- **AI-Powered Intelligence** - Smart recommendations and workflow optimization
- **Enterprise-Grade Security** - Bank-level security with comprehensive audit trails
